Design a parking lot management module with inputs: 1-bit clock clk, reset rst, 1-bit vehicle entry enter, 1-bit vehicle exit exit, and outputs: 4-bit remaining parking space count, 1-bit full flag full. The parking lot capacity is 10 vehicles. enter=1 increases the vehicle count, exit=1 decreases the vehicle count, and count displays the remaining spaces. module_name is "parking_lot_manager". input pin: clk, rst, enter, exit. output pin: count[3:0], full.
