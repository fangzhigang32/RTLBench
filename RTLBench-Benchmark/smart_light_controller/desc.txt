Design a smart home lighting controller module with inputs: 1-bit clock clk, reset rst, 1-bit manual switch switch, 1-bit light sensor light_sensor, and outputs: 1-bit light switch light_on, 3-bit brightness level. When switch=1, manual control is enabled; when light_sensor=1, level is automatically adjusted (0-7). module_name is "smart_light_controller". input pin: clk, rst, switch, light_sensor. output pin: light_on, level[2:0].
