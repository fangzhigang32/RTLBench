Design a 4-floor elevator controller module with inputs: 1-bit clock clk, reset rst, 4-bit floor request floor_req, 2-bit current floor cur_floor, and outputs: 1-bit door switch door, 2-bit target floor next_floor, 1-bit movement direction dir (1=up, 0=down). Move to the target floor based on requests, where door=1 indicates the door is open. module_name is "elevator_controller_4floor". input pin: clk, rst, floor_req[3:0], cur_floor[1:0]. output pin: door, next_floor[1:0], dir.
