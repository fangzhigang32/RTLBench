Design a vending machine controller module with inputs: 1-bit clock clk, reset rst, 2-bit coin input signal coin (00=none, 01=1 yuan, 10=2 yuan), 2-bit item selection item_sel; outputs: 1-bit dispense signal dispense, 2-bit change signal change. The item prices are 3 yuan and 5 yuan respectively. Dispense the item and provide change when sufficient coins are inserted. module_name is "vend_controller". input pin: clk, rst, coin[1:0], item_sel[1:0]. output pin: dispense, change[1:0].
