Design a subway ticket controller module with inputs: 1-bit clock clk, reset rst, 2-bit ticket price ticket (1-3 yuan), 2-bit coin input coin (00=none, 01=1 yuan, 10=2 yuan), and outputs: 1-bit gate opening signal gate, 1-bit change signal change. The gate opens and change is given when sufficient coins are inserted. module_name is "subway_ticket_controller". input pin: clk, rst, ticket[1:0], coin[1:0]. output pin: gate, change.
